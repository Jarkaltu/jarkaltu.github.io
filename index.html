About Us

Mija Group is a small manufacturing and trading business specializing in textile products and the fashion industry. We are dedicated to delivering high-quality products and exceptional service to our clients.

Our Services

We offer a range of services including textile manufacturing, brand representation, and market distribution. Our expertise lies in understanding the fashion trends and meeting the market demands efficiently.

Our Brands

We own the brands Hoffmann and Beck, PAUL and proudly represent several brands from France and Italy in the Baltic market. Our portfolio showcases a blend of quality and style, catering to diverse customer preferences.
